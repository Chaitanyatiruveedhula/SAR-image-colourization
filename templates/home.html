What are SAR Images?

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) images are used to capture detailed images of the Earth's surface, typically for applications in remote sensing, environmental monitoring, and disaster management. These images are often in grayscale due to the radar's nature, which doesn't capture color information. Our tool applies advanced colorization techniques to enhance these grayscale images, helping you visualize the data in a more intuitive and aesthetic way.
